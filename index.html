Rock Paper and Scissors

Humans have been decimated by Aliens. The aliens have gotten rid of everything humans can use as a weapon against them. And now they want us to play a humiliating Rock Paper and Scissors against each other. The winner gets to join them in their conquest of other worlds, and the loser gets to suffer in the now barren wasteland of Earth. Your fate depends on your choices. What do you choose?
